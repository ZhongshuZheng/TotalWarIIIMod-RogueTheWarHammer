# [HT] RogueTotalWar: Warhammer Spire | 战锤尖塔

> A Slay-the-Spire-inspired rogue-lite campaign mode for Total War: Warhammer III.

你是否已经厌倦了每局战役动辄数十小时的经营、扩张与推图流程？  
你是否无数次想开启一局新战役，却又被漫长的战役节奏劝退？

你是否觉得游戏中许多过渡兵种如同鸡肋：明明设计很有趣，却因为招募建筑独立、替代兵种更优、成型速度太慢等原因，几乎从未真正使用过？

你是否希望在《Total War: Warhammer III》中体验一种类似商队系统的 rogue-lite 玩法：快速进入战斗，持续获得奖励，不断构筑部队，并挑战越来越强的敌人？

那么，你来对地方了。

**Warhammer Spire | 战锤尖塔** 正是这样一款为《Total War: Warhammer III》提供 rogue-lite 战役体验的 Mod。它的灵感来自《Slay the Spire》与原版震旦商队系统。

在本 Mod 中，玩家不需要进行传统的招兵、造建筑、经营城镇或大地图扩张，而是通过一连串事件与选项推进游戏流程：选择路线、构筑部队、获得随机奖励、积累战力成长，并不断挑战更加强大的敌军。

---

## 核心玩法

- 基于原版战役植入，但提供一套几乎独立于原战役进程的游玩体验。  
  一切的开端，来自屏幕右下角新增的 **“战锤尖塔”** 按钮。

- 通过连续的决策事件参与战斗，并获得部队、英雄、神器与装备奖励。  
  你无需进行任何传统地图经营，就可以持续推进战斗与成长。

- 在游玩过程中，你有机会获得几乎所有种族的领主、英雄与兵种，组建属于你的超级梦之队。

- 每轮战斗后部队会完全恢复。  
  每场战斗都可以全力以赴，无需担心战损、补员与长期消耗。

- 本 Mod 使用原版多人对战模式中的单位价格作为平衡基础，提供接近 30 轮的相对公平战斗体验。

- 支持通过 MCT 调整部分难度参数。

---

## 玩法循环

1. 进入原版战役，选择任意你喜欢的种族与派系。  
   该种族会成为你的主种族，并影响未来部队与装备奖励的基础方向。

2. 开始游戏后，点击屏幕右下角新增的两把交叠之剑按钮，启动 **战锤尖塔** 玩法。

3. 随机生成一支事件用军队，并将其移动到合适的作战位置，开始事件链。

4. 组建部队构筑，参与战斗并取得胜利，获得单位、装备、经验与英雄成长奖励。

5. 跟随事件指引，前往不同种族节点，面对不同主题的敌军，并取得对应种族的奖励。

6. 战斗，奖励，再战斗，再奖励……直到你抵达更深层的挑战。

---

## 当前版本内容

- 支持至 **珀希瓦（Bhashiwa）版本** 的几乎全部主要种族与派系。

- 游戏事件中会提供原版游戏内绝大多数领主、英雄、普通兵种、精英单位，以及大部分装备与神器奖励。

---

## 兼容性

- 仅支持单人游玩。

- 支持存档，无需开新档。  
  不过由于本 Mod 的主要玩法几乎独立于原战役进程，仍然推荐开新档游玩。

- 支持 MCT，并提供部分可调整选项。

- 已确认兼容：

  - Community Bug Fix Mod
  - Memreader
  - Camera Mod
  - Helper UI

- 不会与 MIXER Unlocker 冲突。  
  但本 Mod 暂时无法使用由 Unlocker 解锁的额外派系。实际上，本 Mod 的主要玩法与具体派系关系较小，更多取决于所选种族。

- 其他 Mod 中新增的领主、英雄、单位与道具，暂时不会出现在本 Mod 的奖励池中。

- 可能不兼容会大幅调整原版物品、装备或单位数据的 Mod。

- 可能与部分修改右下角轮盘按钮区域的 UI Mod 发生冲突。

---

## 已知问题

- 部分《Total War: Warhammer I》时期的传奇领主，例如卡尔·弗兰兹，人物模型可能无法被正确读取，并自动使用“绿皮萨满”替代模型。  
  目前属性、技能树与战斗能力均正常，但模型问题暂未解决。

- 在游玩后期，尤其是 30 层之后，进入战斗前可能出现一定卡顿。

- 仍存在少量暂未能稳定复现的游戏崩溃问题。

# [HT] RogueTotalWar: Warhammer Spire

> A Slay-the-Spire-inspired rogue-lite campaign mode for Total War: Warhammer III.

Are you tired of campaign runs that take dozens of hours of empire management, expansion, and map-painting?  
Have you ever wanted to start a new campaign, only to be discouraged by how long the full campaign experience can become?

Have you ever felt that many mid-tier or transitional units are rarely worth using?  
Some units have interesting designs, but because of separate recruitment buildings, better alternatives, or slow campaign progression, they often never get a real chance to shine.

Do you want a rogue-lite mode for Total War: Warhammer III that plays more like the caravan system: fast battles, frequent rewards, constant army-building, and escalating challenges?

Then you are in the right place.

**Warhammer Spire** is a rogue-lite campaign experience for Total War: Warhammer III, inspired by *Slay the Spire* and the original Cathay caravan system.

In this mod, you do not need to recruit units manually, construct buildings, manage settlements, or paint the campaign map. Instead, you progress through a chain of events and choices: choose your path, build your army, gain random rewards, grow stronger, and keep challenging increasingly powerful enemy armies.

---

## Core Features

- Built into the original campaign, but designed as an almost independent gameplay experience.  
  Everything begins with the new **Warhammer Spire** button in the lower-right corner of the campaign UI.

- Progress through a chain of decision events, fight battles, and earn units, heroes, artifacts, and equipment rewards.  
  No traditional campaign management is required.

- During a run, you may obtain lords, heroes, and units from almost every race, allowing you to build your own dream army.

- Your army fully recovers after each battle.  
  Every fight is meant to be played at full strength, without worrying about casualties, replenishment, or long-term attrition.

- The mod uses the original multiplayer unit prices as the basis for balance, aiming to provide a relatively fair combat experience for around 30 rounds.

- MCT support is included for adjusting several difficulty-related options.

---

## Gameplay Loop

1. Enter a normal campaign and choose any faction from any race you like.  
   Your chosen race becomes your main race and influences the baseline of your future unit and equipment rewards.

2. Once the campaign begins, click the new crossed-swords button in the lower-right corner of the screen to start **Warhammer Spire**.

3. A special event army will be generated. Move it to a suitable position on the campaign map and begin the event chain.

4. Build your army, fight battles, win rewards, gain experience, level up your heroes, and equip powerful artifacts.

5. Follow the event chain to visit different race nodes, face themed enemy armies, and earn rewards associated with those races.

6. Fight, earn rewards, fight again, earn more rewards... and see how deep your run can go.

---

## Current Version Content

- Supports almost all major races and factions up to the **Bhashiwa** version.

- Event rewards include most vanilla lords, heroes, regular units, elite units, and the majority of vanilla equipment and artifact rewards.

---

## Compatibility

- Single-player only.

- Save-game compatible. A new campaign is not required.  
  However, since this mod’s main gameplay is almost entirely independent from normal campaign progression, starting a fresh campaign is still recommended.

- MCT is supported, with several adjustable options.

- Confirmed compatible with:

  - Community Bug Fix Mod
  - Memreader
  - Camera Mod
  - Helper UI

- Does not conflict with MIXER Unlocker.  
  However, factions unlocked by Unlocker are not currently supported by this mod. In practice, Warhammer Spire is mostly based on race selection rather than specific playable factions.

- Lords, heroes, units, and items added by other mods will not appear in Warhammer Spire reward pools for now.

- May be incompatible with mods that heavily alter vanilla item, equipment, or unit data.

- May conflict with UI mods that modify the lower-right campaign button area.

---

## Known Issues

- Some Legendary Lords from Total War: Warhammer I, such as Karl Franz, may fail to load their correct character model and instead use a Greenskin Shaman placeholder model.  
  Their stats, skills, and combat functionality are currently working as expected, but the model issue has not yet been resolved.

- In the late game, especially after floor 30, there may be some stuttering before entering battle.

- A small number of crash bugs may still exist and have not yet been reproduced consistently.
